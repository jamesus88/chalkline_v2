Lost Password

  • Passwords can be reset in case you have forgotten it or you believe your account may be vulnerable.
  • Click Reset Password on the login page.
  • Enter your email and click Submit
  • Note: You can only request one password reset at a time!
  • Login to your email service and locate the email from Chalkline
  • Follow the link in the email to reset your password
  • Never share your password over email or text!
  • Once you enter a new password, proceed to the Login page.
  • If you have issues resetting your password, contact an administrator.
