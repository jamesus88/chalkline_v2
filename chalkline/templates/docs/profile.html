Set Up Profile

Go to My Profile

Teams

  • If you are a Coach or a Parent, you can add teams to your account.
  • This allows you to view the team schedule and/or request youth umpires.
  • Select a team code, then click Add Team.
  • Your team should show under the My Teams heading.
  • To remove teams from your profile, find your team, then click Remove.

Personal Information

  • To edit your personal information, change your first name, last name, email, or phone number.
  • You can also opt-in or opt-out of email/text reminders and alerts.
  • To hide your email or phone from other users, select Hide my email/phone from other users.
  • By default, your email and phone will be available for others in your league to view.
  • Then click Save to update changes.

Session

  • To sign out, click Sign Out.
  • If changes have been made to your account, you may need to sign out and login again.
