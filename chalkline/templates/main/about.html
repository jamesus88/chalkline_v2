About Chalkline

What is Chalkline?

Chalkline, your all-in-one website for everything Little League Baseball.

Chalkline incorporates scheduling, umpiring, coaching, and league services into one, simple webapp.

Schedule practices, games, and umpires easily with one click.

Master schedules are easily accessible and custom views are available for each user.

Teams can view the standings and their upcoming games or practices.

Umpiring made simple.

Chalkline allows umpires to schedule themselves, without the need for an assignor.

Leagues can assign games manually, or let umpires choose their own schedule.

Coaches can request field umpires to fill their umpire duty.

Never miss an update.

Coaches can send announcments to their entire team.

League administrators can send game/field updates to all users.

Always improving.

Updates with new features are pushed out every week.
