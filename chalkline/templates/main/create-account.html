Which roles(s) should I choose?

Each role customizes your Chalkline™ experience.

Coaches can view their team's practice, game, and umpire schedule. They can also request youth umpires and sned messages to their team via email/text.

Parents can view their team's practice, game, and umpire schedule.

Umpires can sign up for plate/field duties and view upcoming games.

Youth Umpires can sign up for coach-requested duties and view upcoming games.

Board Members can sign up for Director on Duty shifts, make reports, view the schedule, and contact umpires/coaches.

Admins can edit data and send out mass messages. Contact administrator for access.
